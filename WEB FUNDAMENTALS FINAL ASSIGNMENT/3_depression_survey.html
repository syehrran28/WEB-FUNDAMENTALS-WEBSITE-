Who Is This Depression Survey For?

The questions are about life experiences that are common among people who suffer from depression.
Please carefully read each question and indicate how frequently you have faced the same or similar challenges in the last few weeks.

Questions Answers
1. There is little interest or enjoyment in doing things.
2. How often have you been bothered by feeling down, depressed, irritable, or hopeless over the last two weeks?
3. How often have you been bothered by trouble falling asleep, staying asleep, or sleeping too much over the last two weeks?
4. How frequently have you been bothered by a lack of appetite, weight loss, or overeating in the last two weeks?
5. How frequently have you been bothered by tiredness or a lack of energy in the last two weeks?
6. How many times in the last two weeks have you felt bad about yourself, like you're a failure, or like you've let yourself or your family down?
7. How often in the last two weeks have you been bothered by the inability to concentrate on things like schoolwork, reading, or watching TV?
8. How often have you been bothered by moving or speaking slowly enough for others to notice? Or have you been fidgety or restless for the last two weeks, moving around a lot more than usual?
9. How often have you been bothered by thoughts that you would be better off dead, or of hurting yourself in some way over the last two weeks?
10. Did you learn and gain any beneficial knowlwdge from our website?

Your privacy is important to us.
All results are completely anonymous.
